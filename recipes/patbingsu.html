Patbingsu

Description

Patbingsu is a popular Korean shaved ice dessert, traditionally made with finely shaved ice topped with sweet red bean paste (pat). This refreshing treat often includes additional toppings such as condensed milk, fruits, jelly, rice cakes, and sometimes ice cream. Patbingsu is a beloved summer delicacy, known for its cool, sweet, and varied texture, offering a delightful way to beat the heat.
  1. Prepare the Ice: Finely shave or crush ice using a blender or ice shaver until it reaches a fluffy, snow-like consistency.
  2. Assemble the Base: Place the shaved ice into a bowl, shaping it into a mound.
  3. Add Red Bean Paste: Spoon sweetened red bean paste (pat) over the top of the shaved ice.
  4. Include Toppings: Add your favorite toppings such as sliced fruits (e.g., strawberries, mango, or kiwi), chewy rice cakes (tteok), jellies, and a drizzle of condensed milk.
  5. Finish with Ice Cream: Top it off with a scoop of ice cream or a sprinkle of nuts for added flavor and texture.
